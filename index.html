Hi, we're
The Dyno Shop.

Building, Tuning, and Repair - We do it all. We have spent many years tuning and repairing all types of motorcycles, from sportbikes to cruisers. All the while sharpening our skills to be able to provide with you the best service possible. We are equipped with the latest and most advanced Dynomometer from Dynojet to provide the best Tuning service for our customers. Oil changes, suspension rebuilds, carburetor and fuel-injection services, ECU Flashes, suspension re-valves, intake and exhaust modifications, or anything else you can think of, we can do for you. ​

The bottom line is, if you like Powersports, you will love our facility.

Honesty, Integrity, and Experience

We Strive For Professionalism, Always.

The owner and operator of our shop has many years as the lead technician in a powersports dealership service and repair setting. He has seen it all over the years, with his wide variety of technical experience between sportbikes, cruisers, off-road vehicles, and any other type of motorcycle.

We Have The Best Dynamometer That Is Available

We knew when the shop was being built that we wanted the absolute best Dynamometer (Dyno) that money could buy. Because we have this powerful machine in house, we have the ability to conduct far more than the typical power and torque measurements that other shops can do.

We Have The Capability For Any Job

If your machine is not in need of an ECU Flash or Tune, then we can perform any other service or repair you may need. Are you thinking of an upcoming track day? We can get your bike race ready. Need new tires but you don't have the tools to do the job yourself? Give us a call.

No Need To Wait Forever!

If you have ever tried to schedule a service on your bike at a big name dealer, then you know how frustrating the wait times can be. Here at The Dyno Shop, we do everything we can to go above and beyond your expectations not only on the quality of work we do, but on how quickly you can be back out riding with a smile on your face.

Recent Projects

2016 Ninja ZX10R

This bike gained a full twenty horsepower throughout the entire rev range, while still on pump gas and still with the stock air system. The only power oriented modifications that we did for this bike was the addition of a slip-on exhaust system, an ECU flash to remove restrictions, and a custom tune for this specfic customer and his riding styles. This bike is a great example of the fact that you do not necessarily need to spend thousands in aftermarket upgrades for massive power gains.

2015 GSXR1000

Because this bike is purely a track machine, the work we did on it was somewhat unique. Since this bike would never be on the street, the performance upgrades were turned up to the max. A full aftermarket exhuast system, race ready bodywork and tires, and a custom tune that makes this bike so fierce that even attempting to ride it on the street would be insane. And, because this build turned out as good as it did, Jake is planning on implementing an updated tune for this bike, but this time meticulously tuning each cylinder one by one to milk out every last drop of power.
